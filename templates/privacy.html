Privacy Policy

Last updated • April 2026

What We Collect +
We collect basic information such as your name, email address, and mobile number when you create an account. Additionally, we collect property details you provide such as location, price, images, and descriptions to help display listings properly on the platform.
Why We Collect +
Your data helps us connect buyers and sellers efficiently. It also allows us to improve user experience, personalize listings, and ensure smooth communication between users through chat and notifications.
What We Do NOT Do +
We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Your information is never shared publicly without your consent. We strictly maintain privacy and ensure that your data is used only within the platform.
Security +
We implement secure authentication systems and encrypted communication wherever possible. Your login details are protected, and all interactions within the platform are kept private and secure.
User Control +
You have full control over your data. You can edit your profile, update property details, or delete your account anytime. We believe in giving users complete control over their information.
