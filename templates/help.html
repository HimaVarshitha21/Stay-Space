Quick Help
Can't upload property?
If you are unable to upload a property, first check that all required fields such as location, price, and description are filled correctly. Make sure you have uploaded at least one image, as properties without images may not be accepted. Also check your internet connection, as slow or unstable networks can interrupt uploads. If the issue still continues, try refreshing the page or logging out and logging back in.
Chat not working?
If the chat feature is not working, ensure that you are logged in and have an active internet connection. Try refreshing the page or reopening the chat window. Sometimes delays can occur due to server response time. If messages are not sending or loading, please wait a few seconds and try again. If the problem persists, report the issue through the Help Center.
Edit profile issue?
If you are facing issues while editing your profile, check that all fields are entered correctly and no invalid data is submitted. Ensure your image size is within allowed limits when uploading a profile picture. If changes are not saving, try refreshing the page and submitting again. If the issue still exists, you can report it using the "Report Problem" option in this page.
FAQs
How to add property?
To add a property, go to the home page and click on the "Add Property" option. You will be asked to fill in important details such as location, price, area, and description. Make sure to upload clear images of your property, as listings with images get better visibility. Once all the details are filled correctly, click on submit. Your property will be listed and visible to other users.
How to delete property?
To delete a property, go to your profile page and navigate to "My Listings". You will see all the properties you have added. Click on the delete icon next to the property you want to remove. Once deleted, the property will no longer be visible to other users. Please note that this action cannot be undone.
How to contact owner?
To contact a property owner, open the property you are interested in and click on the "Chat" button. This will open a messaging interface where you can directly communicate with the owner. You can ask questions, negotiate price, or request more details about the property. All conversations are private and secure within the platform.
Why my listing not visible?
If your listing is not visible, it may be due to incomplete details or missing images. Ensure that all required fields are filled correctly and that at least one image is uploaded. Sometimes, there might be a slight delay in displaying new listings. If the issue persists, try refreshing the page or contact support for assistance.
